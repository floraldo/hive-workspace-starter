# Session Close

1. Summarize changes, completion, remaining work, and unresolved risk.
2. Propose touched-project `03-status/status.md` updates with one next action, blockers,
   decisions, and a dated activity line.
3. Use root `03-status/` only for cross-project state, tasks, or decisions.
4. Promote only reviewed knowledge with provenance; keep it in the project unless broad.
5. Show the update plan and ask before writing ambiguous or sensitive material.
6. Verify no secrets or raw personal content entered status or knowledge.
7. End with the single best next action.

Do not publish, send, sync, commit, or change external systems unless separately requested
and approved.
